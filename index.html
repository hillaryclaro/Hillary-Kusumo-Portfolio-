IMAGE EVALUATION

Image 1

04

VIDEO EVALUATION

Video edit evaluation compares two edited versions of the same source video. I assess motion quality, visual stability, audio sync, and continuity. I choose the output that stays closest to the intended edit prompt while maintaining clean output.

Evaluation Criteria

1. Motion smoothness. Video 1 shows stable frame flow with fewer jumps.

2. Frame consistency. Video 1 keeps object shape stable across time.

3. Audio sync. Video 1 aligns sound and motion with higher accuracy.

4. Prompt adherence. Video 1 stays closer to original instruction.

Result

Final decision: Video 1 selected due to higher stability, stronger consistency, and better prompt match.
