Rodeo Annotation Project

Annotated rodeo videos in COCO JSON format. Covered:

  • Barrel Racing: Bounding boxes for horse, rider, barrel, and visible objects at 30fps.
  • Bull/Bareback/Saddle Bronc Riding: Bounding boxes and keypoints for riders, animals, pickup riders, and non-bucking horses.
  • Keypoints: COCO format for persons (head, shoulders, elbows, wrists, hips, knees, ankles) and animals (nose, horns/ears, withers, mid-back, loin, tail base, limb joints, hooves).
  • Edge Cases: Overlapping objects, partial occlusion, motion blur, small objects, riders fallen off.
  • Consistency: Maintain same IDs across frames for objects and keypoints.
