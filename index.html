Image & Video Evaluation Project

In my portfolio, I showcase a project where I applied structured, rubric-driven QA workflows to both images and videos. My role was to evaluate outputs for instruction-following accuracy, visual integrity, and overall usability, ensuring that they met professional standards while remaining relatable to end users.

Image Evaluation

I began with image evaluation, where I compared edited and generated images against prompts. My process included:

  • Reviewing defect catalogs to identify issues such as banding, spotting, dissolving edges, replicated features, and illogical compositions.
  • Assessing instruction compliance while judging visual quality.
  • Avoiding ties and giving clear justification.
  • Selecting the overall best image based on confidence to share publicly.

This helped me judge realism and quality beyond instruction accuracy.

Video Evaluation

I compared videos based on motion quality, audio clarity, and consistency. I selected Video 2 due to smoother motion and better audio sync.
