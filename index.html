VIDEO EVALUATION

In this evaluation, I compared two video submissions for motion quality, audio synchronization, and visual realism. I applied a structured rubric and recorded the results for each metric.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Motion smoothness: Video 1 had better frame-to-frame continuity, avoiding jitter.
  • Frame consistency: Video 1 maintained visual quality across all frames.
  • Audio sync: Video 1 matched sound with action more accurately.
  • Visual realism: Lighting, shadows, and movement were realistic in Video 1.

Rubric

  • 5: Perfect alignment and realism
  • 4: Minor issues
  • 3: Noticeable inconsistencies
  • 2: Major defects
  • 1: Poor quality

Result

Video 1 performed better across all metrics. Motion remained stable, audio matched visuals, and there were no visible distortions.

Final Decision: Video 1 selected due to better motion quality and synchronization.
